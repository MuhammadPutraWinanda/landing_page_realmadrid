About Us

Real Madrid is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain. Founded in 1902 as Madrid Football Club, it traditionally wears a white home kit. The word Real is Spanish for "royal" and was bestowed on the club by King Alfonso XIII in 1920 along with the royal crown on the club's crest. The club has played its matches at the 85,454-capacity Santiago Bernabéu Stadium in central Madrid since 1947.
